Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 24 oz. marinara sauce
  • 16 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until soft.
  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in the marinara sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce.
  8. Layer 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then add 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese.
  10. Repeat the layers twice more, ending with a final layer of sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  11. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  13. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
