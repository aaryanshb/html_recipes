Stuffed Zucchini Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 medium zucchini
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup marinara sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the zucchini in half lengthwise and scoop out the flesh, leaving about a 1/4-inch-thick shell. Chop the flesh and set it aside.
  3. In a skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until browned. Add the onion, garlic, and chopped zucchini flesh, and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in the cooked rice, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Spoon the filling into the zucchini halves and place them in a baking dish.
  6. Pour the marinara sauce over the stuffed zucchini.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the zucchini is tender.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
